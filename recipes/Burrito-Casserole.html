Burrito Casserole

Description

This burrito casserole is a delicious and easy-to-make dish that combines all the flavors of a classic burrito in a convenient casserole form. It's perfect for feeding a crowd or for meal prep!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can corn, drained
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 8 small flour tortillas, cut into strips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add the taco seasoning, black beans, corn, cooked rice, and salsa to the skillet. Stir to combine.
  4. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, layer half of the tortilla strips on the bottom.
  5. Spread half of the beef mixture over the tortillas, then sprinkle with half of the cheese.
  6. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Let cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
